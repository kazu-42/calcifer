# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in Calcifer a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or personal characteristics.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while addressing concrete technical or community impact.
- Welcome questions and different levels of experience.
- Respect privacy. Never pressure anyone to publish credentials, private logs, account details, or personal information.
- Accept maintainers' decisions about scope, security, and provider-policy boundaries.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, doxxing, deliberate intimidation, sustained disruption, or publishing another person's private information are not acceptable. Attempts to use this project for credential theft, account sharing, unauthorized access, quota bypass, or evasion of provider enforcement are also outside this community.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns through the repository's [private reporting form](https://github.com/kazu-42/calcifer/security/advisories/new) and prefix the summary with `[Conduct]`. This is also the project's temporary confidential channel for conduct reports; do not open a public issue containing sensitive details. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, comments, issues, or other participation that violates this code, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

Maintainers will handle reports as privately as practical and avoid conflicts of interest when deciding a response.
